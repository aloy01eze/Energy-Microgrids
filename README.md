# Decentralized Energy Microgrids

A Clarity smart contract solution allowing rural communities to trade locally-produced solar energy using the Stacks blockchain, enabling those with solar panels to sell excess electricity to neighbors through automated payment and distribution contracts.

## Overview

This project implements a decentralized energy trading platform where community members can register as either energy producers or consumers. Producers can list their available energy and set prices, while consumers can purchase energy directly from producers with transparent pricing and automated payments.

## Features

- **Producer/Consumer Registration**: Users can register as either energy producers or consumers
- **Energy Listing**: Producers can add available energy and set/update prices
- **Direct Purchases**: Consumers can buy energy directly from specific producers
- **Transparent Pricing**: All energy prices are publicly visible on the blockchain
- **Automated Payments**: Smart contract handles payments and fee distribution
- **Transaction History**: All energy transactions are recorded on-chain
- **Low Platform Fee**: Minimal platform maintenance fee (0.5% default)
